Cloud Strife

Cloud is a former member of the elite military unit SOLDIER who becomes a mercenary. He joins a group of rebels, AVALANCHE, to fight against the megacorporation Shinra, which is exploiting the planet's resources. As the story unfolds, Cloud's past and his connection to the game's central villain, Sephiroth, are revealed, making his journey one of self-discovery, redemption, and saving the world.

Squall Leonhart

Squall is a shy, introverted student at the Balamb Garden, a military academy for mercenaries. He becomes the leader of a group of warriors who must stop an evil sorceress, Ultimecia, from altering time and space. Squall's journey is one of personal growth, as he learns to open up emotionally and embrace leadership, while also dealing with themes of love, friendship, and fate.

Tidus

Tidus is a star blitzball player from the city of Zanarkand who is mysteriously transported to the world of Spira after his city is destroyed by a creature called Sin. Throughout the game, Tidus joins Yuna, a summoner, on her pilgrimage to defeat Sin. As the story unfolds, Tidus grapples with the truth about his existence and the fate of Spira, while developing a deep bond with Yuna and her companions. His story is central to the game’s themes of sacrifice, love, and breaking the cycle of despair.

Cecil Harey

He's a dark knight and captain of the Red Wings, the airship fleet of the kingdom of Baron. Struggling with guilt over his orders and actions, Cecil embarks on a journey of redemption. Along the way, he transforms into a paladin, symbolizing his shift from darkness to light. His story is central to the game, dealing with themes of identity, loyalty, and inner conflict.

Vaan

Vaan is a young, aspiring adventurer from the city of Rabanastre, who gets caught up in a struggle for power after his city is attacked. While Vaan is often considered the central protagonist, the story focuses on a broader narrative involving characters like Ashe, the rightful princess of Dalmasca, and Balthier, a charming sky pirate. Vaan’s role is central in the early stages, but the plot develops with a wider focus on politics, war, and the fate of the world.

Lightning

Lightning is a former soldier who becomes the central figure in the fight against the gods and the fate imposed on the people of Cocoon. She is a strong-willed, determined woman who embarks on a journey to save her sister, Serah, and prevent the destruction of her world. Throughout the game, Lightning's story unfolds alongside a diverse group of companions, with themes of destiny, sacrifice, and self-discovery at the heart of the narrative.

Noctis Lucis Caelum

Noctis is the prince of the Kingdom of Lucis, and the game follows his journey as he embarks on a road trip with his close friends and protectors—Gladio, Ignis, and Prompto—to reclaim his throne after his kingdom is attacked. Throughout the game, Noctis grows as a leader and learns to balance his responsibilities as a ruler with his personal struggles, all while uncovering a deeper conspiracy that threatens his world. The game emphasizes themes of friendship, destiny, and sacrifice.

Clive Rosfield

He starts as the First Shield, sworn to protect his younger brother, Joshua, the Dominant of Phoenix. After a catastrophic event that leads to Joshua’s death and Clive's own transformation into the Dominant of Ifrit, Clive embarks on a journey for revenge against those who destroyed his family and homeland. His quest evolves into a broader mission to end the Blight ravaging the world of Valisthea.
