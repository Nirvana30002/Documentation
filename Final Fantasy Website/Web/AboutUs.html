About Us


Welcome to Final Fantasy Chronicles, your ultimate fan-made guide to the Final Fantasy universe! If you’ve ever found yourself wondering how the various games in the franchise connect — or if they even do at all — or if you’ve been curious about the characters, the lore, and the unique gameplay styles across the series, then you’re in the right place. With over 16 mainline titles and numerous spin-offs, Final Fantasy can be overwhelming for both new players and seasoned veterans. Every entry in the series tells a new, expansive story with its own set of characters, settings, and gameplay mechanics. This vastness can sometimes make it hard to understand how each game fits into the larger Final Fantasy world — or whether they’re all part of the same continuity. Our mission is to bridge that gap by offering clear, concise, and in-depth information on each game in the franchise, breaking down the complex narrative threads and offering context to all the rich characters and their roles. At Final Fantasy Chronicles, we go beyond simple summaries. Our content delves deeply into the core themes, overarching narratives, and intricate relationships between characters. We also explore the unique gameplay mechanics of each game, from the early turn-based battle systems to the more action-oriented combat of later entries, helping players understand how the gameplay evolved over time. Each article, guide, and entry we produce is crafted to give you an all-encompassing look at the game’s world and story, helping you appreciate the intricate design and craftsmanship behind the franchise.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a space where both newcomers and veterans can learn, explore, and deepen their appreciation for Final Fantasy. We strive to create the most comprehensive resource for fans of all levels, offering you the tools you need to understand and appreciate each game on its own terms while also seeing how it fits into the greater saga. Whether you’re revisiting a classic, discovering a hidden gem, or exploring the series for the first time, we’re here to guide you through every aspect of Final Fantasy with care, passion, and a commitment to quality content. Join us on this journey through one of the most beloved and expansive video game franchises of all time — and let’s explore the world of Final Fantasy together!
