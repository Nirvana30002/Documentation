Bartz Klauser


Bartz Klauser is the free-spirited, kind-hearted protagonist of Final Fantasy V, whose personal journey from a carefree wanderer to a legendary Warrior of Light is marked by growth, grief, and heroic resolve. Orphaned at a young age and raised by his father, Dorgann, Bartz inherits his father’s love of adventure and the open road, traveling the world with only his loyal chocobo companion, Boco, and no fixed destination. His story begins with a chance encounter near a fallen meteor, where he meets Lenna, Galuf, and later Faris, unknowingly setting into motion his role in a cosmic conflict to protect the Crystals that maintain the balance of the world. Though initially reluctant and lacking a clear purpose beyond exploration, Bartz is gradually drawn into a much greater destiny as the Crystals shatter and an ancient evil—Exdeath, a nihilistic warlock born from a sealed Void—begins to reawaken. Unlike the often brooding or duty-bound protagonists of earlier entries, Bartz’s charm lies in his humanity: he’s cheerful, goofy, and relatable, but beneath his light-hearted demeanor lies a strong sense of justice and deep emotional depth, especially following the loss of his father and the death of Galuf. His father, one of the original Warriors of Dawn, left Bartz not only a legacy of courage but also a direct connection to the ongoing battle against Exdeath, giving Bartz’s role a generational weight. Mechanically, Bartz is the embodiment of the game’s revolutionary Job System, a blank slate who can master any role—Knight, Ninja, Black Mage, Summoner, and more—reflecting his adaptability and the player’s freedom to shape him into a true jack-of-all-trades. As the story expands to include multiple worlds, legendary weapons, and the existential threat of the Void, Bartz matures into a true hero: not one chosen by prophecy, but one who chooses to fight because it’s right. His unshakable optimism, courage in the face of universal annihilation, and loyalty to his friends make Bartz a uniquely grounded yet epic protagonist, whose legacy is one of heart, freedom, and friendship.
