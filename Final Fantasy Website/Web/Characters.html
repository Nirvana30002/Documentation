Warriors of Light

The Warriors of Light are four heroes chosen by prophecy to restore balance to the world in Final Fantasy I. Each carries a darkened crystal tied to one of the four elements—earth, fire, water, and wind. Though unnamed and customizable, they represent hope and courage, journeying across the land to defeat the elemental fiends and bring light back to the crystals.

Firion

One of four orphans who join the rebellion against the Palamecian Empire after their home is destroyed. Firion is the first named protagonist in the Final Fantasy series and is known for his determination, loyalty, and strong sense of justice as he fights to free the world from tyranny.

Crystal of Light

The Crystal of Light is a powerful, mystical artifact that chooses four heroes to restore balance to the world. It represents the forces of light and order, guiding the chosen ones to stop the spread of darkness. The crystal grants them the ability to change jobs, empowering them to face the challenges ahead and protect the world from destruction.

Cecil Harey

He's a dark knight and captain of the Red Wings, the airship fleet of the kingdom of Baron. Struggling with guilt over his orders and actions, Cecil embarks on a journey of redemption. Along the way, he transforms into a paladin, symbolizing his shift from darkness to light. His story is central to the game, dealing with themes of identity, loyalty, and inner conflict.

Bartz Klauser

Bartz is a carefree adventurer who gets caught up in a quest to protect the elemental crystals after a meteor crashes near his path. Alongside his companions—Lenna, Galuf, and Faris—he travels the world to stop an ancient evil. Bartz’s lighthearted nature and bravery make him the heart of the group, and his journey ties deeply into the game's themes of friendship, fate, and legacy.

Terra Branford

As one of the first characters you play as, Terra’s struggle to understand her identity and emotions drives much of the early story. However, FFVI features a large ensemble cast, and other characters like Celes, Locke, and Kefka (the villain) also play major roles. Still, Terra is widely seen as the emotional and narrative center of the game.

Cloud Strife

Cloud is a former member of the elite military unit SOLDIER who becomes a mercenary. He joins a group of rebels, AVALANCHE, to fight against the megacorporation Shinra, which is exploiting the planet's resources. As the story unfolds, Cloud's past and his connection to the game's central villain, Sephiroth, are revealed, making his journey one of self-discovery, redemption, and saving the world.

Squall Leonhart

Squall is a shy, introverted student at the Balamb Garden, a military academy for mercenaries. He becomes the leader of a group of warriors who must stop an evil sorceress, Ultimecia, from altering time and space. Squall's journey is one of personal growth, as he learns to open up emotionally and embrace leadership, while also dealing with themes of love, friendship, and fate.

Zidane Tribal

Zidane is a cheerful and mischievous thief who works for a theater troupe. He becomes involved in a quest to stop the evil queen Brahne, who seeks to destroy the world. Zidane's journey is not only about saving the world but also discovering his true origins, identity, and the importance of friendship and sacrifice. His lighthearted nature contrasts with the more serious themes of the game, which centers on themes of life, death, and purpose.

Tidus

Tidus is a star blitzball player from the city of Zanarkand who is mysteriously transported to the world of Spira after his city is destroyed by a creature called Sin. Throughout the game, Tidus joins Yuna, a summoner, on her pilgrimage to defeat Sin. As the story unfolds, Tidus grapples with the truth about his existence and the fate of Spira, while developing a deep bond with Yuna and her companions. His story is central to the game’s themes of sacrifice, love, and breaking the cycle of despair.

Vaan

Vaan is a young, aspiring adventurer from the city of Rabanastre, who gets caught up in a struggle for power after his city is attacked. While Vaan is often considered the central protagonist, the story focuses on a broader narrative involving characters like Ashe, the rightful princess of Dalmasca, and Balthier, a charming sky pirate. Lightning

Warrior of Light

The Warrior of Light is a customizable protagonist who becomes the central figure in the fight against various threats to the world of Eorzea. Depending on the player's chosen race, class, and personal decisions, the Warrior of Light's appearance and abilities can vary. Throughout the game's expansive storylines, the Warrior of Light plays a pivotal role in defeating powerful enemies and protecting the realm from various forces, including primal beings, corrupted deities, and dark factions.

Noctis Lucis Caelum

Noctis is the prince of the Kingdom of Lucis, and the game follows his journey as he embarks on a road trip with his close friends and protectors—Gladio, Ignis, and Prompto—to reclaim his throne after his kingdom is attacked. Throughout the game, Noctis grows as a leader and learns to balance his responsibilities as a ruler with his personal struggles, all while uncovering a deeper conspiracy that threatens his world. The game emphasizes themes of friendship, destiny, and sacrifice.

Clive Rosfield

His quest evolves into a broader mission to end the Blight ravaging the world of Valisthea.
