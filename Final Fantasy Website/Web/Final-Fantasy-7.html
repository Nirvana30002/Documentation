Final Fantasy 7


Final Fantasy VII takes place in a dystopian world dominated by the Shinra Electric Power Company, a corrupt mega-corporation that exploits the planet’s life force—known as Mako energy—to fuel its cities and weapons. The story begins with Cloud Strife, a stoic and seemingly detached mercenary who joins a rebel group called AVALANCHE, led by the passionate Barret Wallace, in their fight against Shinra’s environmental destruction. Though Cloud claims to be a former member of the elite military unit SOLDIER, his past is clouded by memory issues and identity confusion. As the group sabotages Mako reactors and uncovers Shinra’s dark experiments, they stumble upon a far more existential threat: Sephiroth, a former SOLDIER hero who turns villainous after learning the horrifying truth of his origins. Believing himself to be the heir of an ancient race called the Cetra, Sephiroth seeks to merge with the planet’s core and become a god by summoning a devastating spell known as Meteor. As the party grows—with unforgettable allies like Tifa, Cloud’s childhood friend and moral anchor; Aerith, a mysterious flower girl and the last surviving Cetra; Red XIII, a wise beast-like creature; Vincent, a brooding gunman with a tragic backstory; and more—they travel across a richly imagined world spanning city slums, ancient temples, and forgotten ruins. The emotional core of the story intensifies when Aerith is killed by Sephiroth, a moment that devastated players and underscored the high stakes of the conflict. Along the way, Cloud must confront the truth about who he is, slowly piecing together his fractured memories and accepting that much of his identity was borrowed from others. The climax unfolds in a desperate battle to stop Sephiroth and save the planet from annihilation, as the group races to summon Holy, the only magic powerful enough to counter Meteor. In the final confrontation, Cloud and Sephiroth engage in a symbolic duel of wills, with Cloud emerging victorious by finally reclaiming his true self. Though Meteor strikes the planet, Holy—aided by the planet’s own spirit—ultimately intervenes, and the world begins to heal. Final Fantasy VII is widely praised for its cinematic storytelling, deep psychological themes, environmental message, and unforgettable characters.
